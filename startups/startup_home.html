Aim

A platform to provide visibility to all the current student startups of IIT Bombay amongst the Angel Investors and Venture Capitalist Network. It is a go-to place for all our IITB initiatives and events and a forum to post all your public queries, internship offers and requests that you want to be circulated amongst the students.

Entrepreneurship and Business Club

The EnB Club is an initiative under the umbrella of E-Cell IIT Bombay. It promotes and fosters entrepreneurship in the institute. We aim to cultivate, grow and nurture a healthy and active entrepreneurial ecosystem. Through a series of year-round events and activities, we guide the institute students through their startup journey.
